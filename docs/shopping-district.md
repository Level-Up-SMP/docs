# :shopping_cart: Shopping District

Below are the shopping district information and rules.

## Launch Time

The shopping district will be launched 1-2 weeks after the launch of the server to allow people to get onto the server and establish themselves.

## Permit List

**Link**: [GSheet List](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1OCymqW-eR-S3HEFoNYCgFnNZfes3kvaVTiSOyN5NQ2s/edit?usp=sharing)

## Permit Rules

1. Permits are available on a first-come, first-served basis once released.
2. Each player may hold one permit per tier (tiers: Custom, Diamond, Gold, and Iron).
3. While permits are free, permit holders are expected to contribute to the shopping district — this includes helping clear mycelium and assisting with the development of the area surrounding their shop.
4. Shops that have been inactive for 30 days or more may have their permit reclaimed and their shop removed from the shopping district.


## Shop Plugin

While the Shop plugin is not strictly required, it is HIGHLY encouraged that it be used to prevent any potential theft while also increasing a store owner's awareness of when they are out of stock. If you have not used the Shop plugin before, you can find a tutorial [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6lDM1aAhgv0).

## Permit Requests

Permit requests can be made in the Discord in the #support forum with the `Permit Request` tag. Permits are on a first come, first serve basis and may not be requested or reserved prior to the opening of the shopping district.
