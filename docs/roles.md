# Roles

Below is a list of our current roles, the hierarchy, and a little bit about each.

## Admin

Admins are the decision makers and high-level enforcers of the server. They are responsible for server configuration, policy decisions, and handling situations that go beyond the scope of day-to-day moderation. When major calls need to be made, they make them.

## Moderator

Moderators are the backbone of day-to-day server operations. They handle player reports, issue bans and mutes, manage game modes, and generally keep things running smoothly.

## VIP

VIP is awarded to players who have made meaningful non-monetary contributions to the server or community. This includes things like content creation, community involvement, or simply being a well-known and respected presence. It is not purchased — it is earned.

## Supporter

Supporter is our way of recognizing players who financially support the server. While it can technically be awarded for other forms of support, most non-monetary contributors are recognized with VIP instead. If you'd like to help keep the lights on, this is how.

## Default

Default is the role every player starts with. It represents full membership in the community — no special perks, but everything you need to play, build, and be part of the world.
