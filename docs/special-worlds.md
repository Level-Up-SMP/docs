# Special Worlds

All special worlds are accessed via dedicated portals. As of writing, these portals are only located inside of the nether hub.

## Admin World

In the interest of full disclosure, there is an admin-only world used for testing and other administrative purposes.

## Creative World

The creative world is exactly as it sounds. It is purely a creative world where players can test builds and mechanics, or even just check out some of the blocks all in a shared world.

While we can't commit to _never_ resetting the creative world, it's our intention to leave things in there as-is unless things become too crowded or some unforeseen mishap.

### Creative World Rules

1. Mark your builds/experiments/area(s) with a sign. If it doesn't have a sign, it may be removed.
2. Only take up the space that you need. Not everything needs a large space.
3. Clean up after yourself. If it was just a basic test or experiment that you no longer need, tear it down after.
4. Don't touch other people's stuff. If it's causing you an issue, open a support ticket in the Discord.
5. Don't break the server/world. If you do, your permissions may be permanently revoked.

## Resource World

The resource world exists solely to harvest resources from. There are no mobs of any kind, but environmental hazards like lava and fall damage are still very much present.

The world may be reset or reseeded from time to time depending on community need. This means that anything you leave in this world **WILL** be lost if you don't remove it. Advanced notice will be given prior to reset.

### Resource World Rules

1. This world is for harvesting only. Do not build permanent structures.
2. Do not leave large open excavations, exposed lava, or other hazards behind for other players.
3. Remove your belongings before any announced reset — anything left will be lost.
